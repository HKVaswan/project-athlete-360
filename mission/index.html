What We Envision

  • A future where every child's athletic potential can be identified and nurtured.
  • A system that empowers institutions to build world-class sports programs.
  • A clear, viable, and respected career path for athletes in India.
  • A supportive community connecting athletes, coaches, and industry experts.

What We Are Building

  • Content: Creating a knowledge hub for athletes and coaches.
  • Awareness: Highlighting stories of struggle and success to inspire change.
  • Community: Building a network for collaboration and support.
  • Research: Using data to understand and solve core problems.
  • Education: Developing programs to upskill PE teachers and coaches.
