Everything You Need

A complete platform for
every part of your institute

From enrollment to competition results — PA360 Proton handles every aspect of sports management in one place.

👤

Athlete Profiles

Complete athlete records — personal details, sport, enrollment date, emergency contacts, medical notes, and a unique athlete ID for every member.

📊

Performance Tracking

Log sport-specific metrics for every session. Visual progress charts show improvement over time. 15+ sports supported with predefined metrics.

✅

Attendance Management

Mark daily attendance in seconds. Track streaks, absences, and percentages. Get alerted when athletes fall below your threshold.

🏆

Competition & Events

Log tournament results, mark event winners, and track competition history. A full calendar keeps coaches and athletes always informed.

🏅

Leaderboards & Badges

Motivate athletes with class and institute-wide leaderboards. Automated and coach-awarded badges reward effort and achievement.

📢

Announcements

Send targeted announcements to athletes, coaches, or your entire institute. Everyone stays informed, every time.

📄

Reports & Exports

Generate clean PDF reports for any athlete — attendance, performance, grades, competition history. Perfect for scholarship applications and selection trials.

🔒

Role-Based Access

Four tailored dashboards — Super Admin, Institute Admin, Coach, and Athlete. Everyone sees exactly what they need, nothing more.

📈

Fitness Grading

Assign numeric grades (1–10) for fitness tests alongside performance metrics. A complete picture of every athlete's development.
