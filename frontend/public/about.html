Our Story & Mission

Built for athletes.

Project Athlete 360 exists because too many talented athletes fall through the cracks of a system that was never properly built for them. PA360 Proton is our answer to that problem.

Why We Exist

The sports world has a management problem.

Across India and around the world, sports institutes are managing hundreds of athletes with spreadsheets, paper registers, and WhatsApp groups. Performance data gets lost. Attendance is guessed. Coaches have no visibility into which athletes are improving and which are quietly falling behind.

The tools that exist are either too expensive for most institutes to afford, too complex for coaches to actually use daily, or built for professional clubs — not the grassroots academies where most athletes actually develop.

PA360 Proton was built to solve this. Professional-grade sports management, at a price that makes it accessible to every institute that needs it.

The Founder's Story

A problem seen on a dusty field.

This journey didn't begin in a boardroom. It began on a field — watching a talented young athlete with undeniable ability and nowhere to go. The path forward was invisible, blocked by a lack of structure, guidance, and the right systems around them. That moment planted the seed for everything that followed.

H.K. Vaswan chose Physical Education as a subject in Class 11 — alongside PCM — at a time when most students wouldn't. That early commitment shaped the path forward. He went on to complete a Bachelor of Physical Education, graduating as the first rank holder in his course, and is currently pursuing his Master's. Alongside academics, NCC 'B' and 'C' certification instilled a systems-oriented, disciplined approach that carries through into everything built at Project Athlete 360.

The experience of sport isn't purely academic for him either. As an endurance runner who has competed in long-distance races and half marathons, he understands what it means to train consistently, track progress, and push through barriers — from the inside. That perspective shapes every product decision: features are built for real athletes and real coaches, not hypothetical ones.

We set out to build the system we wish had existed — one that gives every athlete, in every institute, the structure and visibility they deserve to reach their potential.

Having hosted university-level programmes and engaged in competitive speaking and debate, communication and clarity are central to how Project Athlete 360 operates. The platform reflects that: every dashboard, every feature, every piece of copy is designed to be immediately understood — not learned over weeks of onboarding.

The problem the platform addresses is straightforward: India's sports institutes are managing athletes with tools built for offices, not training grounds. Spreadsheets. Paper registers. Group chats. Talent slipping through the gaps because no one has the full picture. PA360 Proton is the direct answer — purposeful, affordable, and built by someone who has been in the sport long enough to know exactly what's missing.

What Drives Us

Principles we build everything around

🎯
Accessibility First
Professional sports management shouldn't be reserved for institutes with large budgets. Every feature we build, every price we set, is guided by the question: can the small academy on a tight budget afford this?
🔬
Science-Backed
Every feature in PA360 Proton is grounded in how sports performance actually works — progressive overload, attendance as a performance metric, data-driven coaching. We don't guess. We build on evidence.
🛡️
Athlete Protection
Athletes — especially young ones — deserve to have their data handled with care. We build privacy and protection into the foundation of the platform, not as an afterthought. Every safeguard is intentional.
⚡
Practical by Design
A coach shouldn't need a training session to mark attendance. The daily workflow must be completable in minutes — not because we simplified the features, but because we thought deeply about how coaches actually work.
📈
Transparency
No hidden fees. No surprise limits. No locked features that only appear after you've signed up. What you see on our pricing page is exactly what you get. We believe trust is earned through honesty.
🌱
Built to Grow
PA360 Proton is built to scale alongside the institutes that use it. From 20 athletes to 2,000 — the platform grows with you. And as we grow, we reinvest in features that matter to our users most.
