You'll receive an alert when you reach 80% of your athlete limit. At 100%, coaches won't be able to enroll new athletes until you upgrade. Existing athletes and all their data are completely unaffected.
Absolutely. We take data protection seriously — especially for minor athletes. Sensitive data like medical notes and emergency contacts are never exportable in bulk. All data access is logged and role-restricted. Coaches can only see their assigned athletes.
Once you sign up as an Institute Admin, you enroll your coaches directly from your dashboard. Coaches then enroll their athletes. Everyone receives login credentials automatically via email — no technical setup required.
Yes. Pro and Elite plans allow you to add custom sports with your own performance metrics. The platform comes with 15+ sports predefined including Cricket, Football, Swimming, Kabaddi, Badminton and more — you just add what you need on top.
If you cancel, your access continues until the end of your billing period. Your data is preserved for 60 days after that — giving you time to resubscribe and pick up exactly where you left off. After 60 days, data is permanently deleted and you'll be notified 7 days in advance.
Annual plans are non-refundable but you can cancel at any time. Your access will continue until the end of the annual period. We recommend starting with a monthly plan first if you're unsure — you can always switch to annual later for the 25% discount.
